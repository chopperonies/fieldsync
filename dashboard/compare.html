LinkCrew vs Jobber vs Housecall Pro

If you're shopping field-service software, you're probably comparing all three. Here's an honest, dated, side-by-side look at price and features — pulled from each company's public pricing page.

Last verified: April 2026 · Sources: getjobber.com · housecallpro.com

The 30-second version

$13/seat
LinkCrew Pro is ~$13/seat-equivalent at 15 seats. Jobber Grow is ~$20/seat at 10 seats. Housecall Pro MAX is ~$64/seat at 10 seats (1 included + 9 add-ons).
Voice bundled
LinkCrew Pro includes the AI voice receptionist. Jobber's Receptionist is a $99/mo add-on (free on $699 Plus). Housecall Pro doesn't offer one on any plan.
No lock-in
LinkCrew's listed prices are month-to-month. Jobber's lowest advertised prices require an annual commitment. Both LinkCrew and HCP offer optional annual discounts.

What you pay at each tier

All prices USD, monthly billing, no commitment. Annual prepay discounts available on all three products.
